Tomato soup

This classic tomato soup recipe is full of bold flavor: easy to make and perfectly seasoned! Serve with grilled cheese for a cozy meal.

Ingredients:

for 4 servings:

  1. Olive oil
  2. Garlic
  3. Canned tomatoes: fire roasted if possible
  4. Vegetable broth
  5. Garlic powder and onion powder
  6. Salt
  7. Butter
