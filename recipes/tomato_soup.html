Tomato soup

This classic tomato soup recipe is full of bold flavor: easy to make and perfectly seasoned! Serve with grilled cheese for a cozy meal.

Ingredients:

for 4 servings:

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes: fire roasted if possible
  • Vegetable broth
  • Garlic powder and onion powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

steps:

  1. Chop & saute: Mince the garlic, then saute in olive oil for 30 seconds.
  2. Simmer: Add the canned tomatoes, broth onion powder, garlic powder, and salt. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Blend: Immersion blend or carefully blend the soup (optional).
  4. Add butter: Add butter and salt to taste. Voila!
